Research Interests. National Security, Just War, religion and ethics

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Civic and Economic Thought and Leadership at Arizona State. Prior to that I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Kinder Institute. I completed my doctoral work at Texas.

Broadly, my work examines US grand strategy and decision-making. More narrowly, it examines strategic leadership, or the intersection of strategy and ethics. My full CV can be found at the above link. Prior to Texas I worked at the Jack Miller Center and am a 12 year veteran of the Air National Guard where I served as a avionics maintainer. I earned my B.A. at The Ohio State University and M.A. at Villanova University.

My personal-family blog is linked for those who know me personally; professional colleagues will find the posts mundane, I suspect. I am (ocassionally) on Twitter, but your best to find me at LinkedIn and Academia.
